1) Open PostMan.
2) Enter Collections Tab.
3) Press Import at the Top of the Right Corner of the Scratch Pad.
4) At the newly opened window Drag & Drop the Project's postman_collection.json File and Press Orange Import button at the bottom of the window.
5) Now you can find all Endpoints for the given Controller in one Place. Also don't hesitate to add new ones!
6) ENJOY! :)
